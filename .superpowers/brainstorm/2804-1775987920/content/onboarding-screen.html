What should the onboarding preferences screen look like?

This is shown once, right after Stripe payment completes. The user picks their states + categories, then lands on the feed.

A

Single page — everything at once

One screen with states, categories, and the 88-day toggle all visible. A "C'est parti!" button at the bottom saves and redirects to the feed. Simple and fast.

B

Two steps — states first, then categories

Step 1: "Where are you looking?" (pick states). Step 2: "What kind of work?" (pick categories + 88-day toggle). Progress bar at top. Feels more guided, less overwhelming.

C

Single page with a skip option

Same as A, but with a "Passer" (Skip) link at the top. If they skip, they get no job notifications until they set preferences later in Settings. Respects users who want to explore first.
